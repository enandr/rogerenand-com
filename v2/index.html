What Is My Developer Story

I was first introduced to coding at a young age, middle school. I participated in and led a lego robotics competitive team. It was there that I learned the basics of programming. Moving ahead to high school, I took a Web Development class focused on HTML and CSS. In that class I discovered my love for programming.

Through the years I have built several personal Websites and Apps as a hobby. As I discovered new technologies, or fun ideas, I taught myself what I needed to know and built a new project or continued a project trying to improve it using the newly gained knowledge.

Recently I went to an intensive Web Development Program called Learning Fuze to push my knowledge forward in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NODE, React, MySql, and PHP.
